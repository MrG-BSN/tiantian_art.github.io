About me.

My name is Tian Tian (Su RuoXi), and I’ve been studying art since I was 3. I have a deep passion for love, and hope to engage in art related work in the future. I have participated in numerous competitions and has won many prizes. For example, in 2019, I won the art competition hosted by the European Union, which had entries from the whole of Nanjing. Another example is that I won the COBIS art competition which had entries all over the world! I also won the bookmark competition in our school for 3 years in a row, and I have also won many zodiac animal drawing competitions from my school. I also like photography. Every week I go out with my grandpa, who also has an passion in photography, to take photos. Vincent Van Gogh is my favourite artist and Starry night is my favourite piece of artwork. I love the style of Impressionism and I really like his use of colours. Art is my favourite hobby and in the future, I hope to achieve more in art and work towards my goal!
